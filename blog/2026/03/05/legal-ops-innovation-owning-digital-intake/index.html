How legal operations and innovation teams can own digital intake (without a dev team)

Buyer personas: innovation lead, legal ops, department head — aligned with Instructd’s self-serve journey story.

Persona: Head of Legal Ops / Innovation

Pain: IT backlog and consultancy lead times; every new campaign or reg change waits in a queue.
Want: Business-owned workflow with guardrails — audit trail, approvals, environments, IT security sign-off once, not per tweak.

Instructd is positioned to let non-dev teams build journeys in a visual editor with low-code power, while CISO-friendly controls (e.g. Entra tenant auth) stay intact.

Persona: Managing partner / HOD

Pain: £250k–£500k-style custom dev for portals with 12–18 month narratives (as per industry comparisons in Instructd’s business materials).
Answer: Productised intake + per-completion economics = capex avoidance and faster time to revenue on new lines.
