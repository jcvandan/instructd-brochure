The modern intake ops loop

  1. Measure funnel steps per journey variant.
  2. Identify drop-off screens and fields.
  3. Experiment with copy order and branching.
  4. Ship changes to dev/test/live with governance not FTP.

Instructd’s roadmap explicitly lists analytics dashboards and A/B testing for journey optimisation — the same playbook insurance growth teams use, applied to matters instead of policies.
