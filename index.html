What is Instructd?

Instructd is online intake for law firms. Instead of clients phoning your contact centre, they complete a guided online journey — like applying for insurance or a mortgage.

The information goes straight into your case management system. At the end, clients can sign up to a portal to track their case progress and communicate with your firm.

You need fewer staff. Your clients get a better experience. And with the call-handler bottleneck gone, you can process more cases and grow revenue. It's a win-win-win.
